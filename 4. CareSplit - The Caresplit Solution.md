## **🏥 CareSplit: Making Healthcare Payments Human-Sized**

**Product Name:** CareSplit

**Timeline:** March 9 – March 15

---

## **1\. The Challenge: Why CareSplit Matters**

In Nigeria, healthcare is a "pay-before-service" world. Most hospitals require a large lump-sum payment upfront, yet most patients earn their money once a month. This "clash" between how we earn and how we pay leads to dangerous consequences.

## **The Real-World Impact:**

* **Medical Suppression:** Patients stay at home and ignore symptoms because they know they can't afford the bill today.  
* **Treatment Interruption:** Patients start a care plan but stop halfway when funds run out.  
* **Financial Trauma:** Families are forced to sell land, livestock, or borrow at predatory interest rates to stay alive.

## **The "Pain Points" We Are Solving:**

* **The "Lump Sum" Barrier:** High upfront costs are the \#1 reason for delayed care.  
* **Clinic Manual Stress:** Most clinics track installments manually (if at all), leading to lost records and missed payments.  
* **The Risk Factor:** Clinics are afraid to offer installments because they have no way to ensure the patient will actually pay.

---

## **2\. Our Solution: Aligning Health with Income**

CareSplit is a digital infrastructure that allows patients to split large medical bills into manageable, automatic monthly payments.

## **Who is this for?**

**Ada, the Lagos Professional.** She earns ₦250,000 a month but is asked for ₦180,000 upfront for a 6-month hypertension plan. She can't pay ₦180,000 today, but she *can* easily pay ₦30,000 a month.

## **Unique Value Proposition:**

* **Not a Loan, but a Facilitator:** We don't charge interest or create "debt shame." We simply manage a split-bill contract between the patient and the clinic.  
* **"Set and Forget":** Automated recurring debits remove the stress of manual transfers.  
* **Trust Indicators:** We use 2026-standard identity verification (BVN/NIN) to ensure both parties are secure.

---

## **3\. How It Works (The Core Experience)**

We’ve prioritized features using the **MoSCoW** method (Must-have vs. Nice-to-have) to ensure a lean, effective launch.

## **The User Journey:**

1. **Onboarding:** Patient signs up securely.  
2. **Plan Creation:** The clinic enters the total bill and creates a care plan in under 3 minutes.  
3. **The Split:** Our logic automatically breaks the bill into monthly installments.  
4. **Authorization:** The patient approves an automated monthly debit via Interswitch.  
5. **Tracking:** Both patient and clinic have dashboards to track progress and treatment adherence.

## **The "Beachhead" Strategy (Phase 1):**

We will start with **Chronic Care** (Hypertension, Diabetes, and Antenatal care) because these patients have the most predictable, recurring needs, making them the perfect fit for installment plans .

---

## **5\. Next Steps & Phase 2 Roadmap**

Once we validate our core model, we will expand CareSplit into a full health-finance ecosystem:

* **Risk Scoring:** Using AI to predict and prevent payment defaults.  
* **Emergency Micro-Loans:** For sudden health shocks that exceed the standard care plan.  
* **Diaspora "Care-Send":** Enabling Nigerians abroad to set up and pay for care plans for parents back home.

**Final Positioning:** CareSplit is the **"Stripe for Healthcare Installments"** in emerging markets, removing the financial "shocks" that stand between Nigerians and their well-being.
