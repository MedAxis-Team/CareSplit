The Nigerian healthcare landscape in 2026 is at a tipping point: out-of-pocket spending has hit 81.92%, and while health insurance coverage is growing, it still only reaches about 13% of the population.

This "missing middle", people like Ada who aren't in the 13% but aren't "poor", is our goldmine.

## **2\. Business Model Overview**

CareSplit operates as a **B2B2C (Business-to-Business-to-Consumer)** platform. We don't lend money; we facilitate a payment contract.

* **Revenue Streams:**  
  * **The Transaction Fee:** A small processing fee (e.g., **1.5% to 2.5%**) per installment, capped at a fair amount.  
  * **The Clinic Subscription:** A monthly SaaS fee for clinics to use the "CareSplit Dashboard" to manage their receivables.  
  * **Premium "Peace of Mind" (Future):** A small monthly fee for patients to get "payment protection" (e.g., if they lose their job, CareSplit covers one month).  
* **The "Zero-Interest" Hook:** Unlike banks, we don't charge interest. We charge for the **infrastructure** that makes paying easier.

## **3\. Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy Outline**

We shouldn't try to "boil the ocean." We start where the pain is sharpest.

* **Phase 1: The "Chronic Care" Beachhead (Months 1–3)**  
  * Target clinics specializing in **Hypertension, Diabetes, and Antenatal care**. These require predictable, recurring visits—the perfect use case for installments.  
* **Phase 2: The "HMO Drop-out" Recovery (Months 4–6)**  
  * Target individuals who recently cancelled their HMO plans due to 2025/2026 premium hikes (which rose up to 59%).  
* **Phase 3: Diaspora "Care-Send" (Months 7+ )**  
  * Enable Nigerians abroad to set up installment plans for their parents’ treatments back home.

## **4\. Partnership Opportunity List**

To scale, we need "Trust Anchors."

* **Payment Infrastructure:** **Interswitch** (Priority \#1). Using their "Verve" and tokenization APIs is non-negotiable for recurring debits in Nigeria.  
* **Clinic Chains:** **Lagoon Hospitals** or **Reddington**. Partnering with one "big name" chain validates the product for smaller clinics.  
* **Pharmaceutical Chains:** **HealthPlus** or **Medplus**. If a patient can split the cost of 6 months of medication, adherence skyrockets.  
* **Identity Verification:** **NIMC (National Identity Management Commission)** via API providers (like Smile ID) to ensure users are real and traceable.
